*about.

Snowthorn was started back in February 2014 with the simple aim of offering experience, advice and expertise to help companies translate business needs into smart software solutions.

However straightforward it sounds, we believe there is more to building solutions than simply choosing the right technology and architecture.

Our experience across the full solution development lifecycle has lead to a more holistic approach to problem solving which considers the needs of your team alongside those of project stakeholders. We can help you learn what is possible and what the right approach should be for your business and your clients.
