*about.

Snowthorn was started back in February 2014 with the simple aim of offering experience, advice and expertise to help companies translate business needs into smart software solutions.

However simple it sounds, we believe there is far more to building solutions than simply choosing the right technology and architecture.

The capabilities of your team to not only write the code but also understand and maintain it are essential to consider. Prescriptive architecture and restrictive business environments can leave a team with their burn down rate flatlining.

Our experience across the full solution development lifecycle leads to a more holistic approach to problem solving. We can help you learn what is possible and what the right approach should be for you and your business.
